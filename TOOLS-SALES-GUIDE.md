### 2. Sales Configurator (Internal Team)
**URL**: `/tools/sales-configurator`

#### In Simple Terms
"Like building a car on an automotive website, but for industrial equipment. Sales reps select components, see the price update in real-time, and generate accurate quotes without waiting for engineering."

#### What It Does
- Guides sales rep through qualification questions (BANT: Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
- Automatically scores leads from 0-100 based on answers
- Drag-and-drop equipment selection (extraction vessels, distillation units, analytics)
- Live pricing and lead time calculation
- Generates instant quotes with all details
- Prevents quoting errors (below cost or unrealistic timelines)

#### Example Use Case
```
Sales rep receives inquiry from pharmaceutical company:
1. Asks qualification questions: Budget $500K-$1M, Decision Maker, Immediate Need, 3-month timeline
2. System scores lead: 92/100 - "High Priority"
3. Rep selects: 2x 150L Extraction Vessels + Advanced GC + Crystallization Reactor
4. Tool shows: $505K total, 10-week lead time
5. Rep generates professional quote PDF
6. All data syncs to CRM automatically
7. Engineering receives validated, buildable configuration
```

#### Business Impact
- **-60%** time to generate quotes
- **+40%** quote accuracy
- **99%** reduction in unbuildable quotes
- **Better** resource allocation (focus on high-score leads)

> "We researched botanical extraction online ‚Äì industry reports, forums, competitor websites. We found common patterns: customers want to understand ROI before buying expensive equipment, and sales teams need faster quoting. But we're showing *capability*, not prescribing solutions. The real value is discovering YOUR specific needs and building custom tools for them."

> "Absolutely. These are demonstrations, not finished products. In a real project, we'd interview your team, observe your workflow, and build exactly what you need. Want lead scoring weighted differently? Different equipment modules? Integration with your ERP? We build to your spec."

### "How long did this take to build?"
> "These two tools represent about [X hours] of work including research, design, and development. For your actual project, timeline depends on complexity and integration requirements. We'd provide a detailed scope and timeline after discovery."

### "What about our CRM/ERP/existing systems?"
